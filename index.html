Flexible Diffusion Modeling of Long Videos

Videos over an hour long sampled on GQN-Mazes and MineRL by iterated application of our Hierarchy-2 sampling scheme, and a CARLA Town 01 video sampled with an autoregressive sampling scheme. We condition on the first 36 frames of a test video. Observed frames are shown with a red border, and we mark the end of the video with a checkerboard pattern.
